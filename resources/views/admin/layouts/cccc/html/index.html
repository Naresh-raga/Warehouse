How It Works

  • Step 1

    Every pet is unique. Tell us a few details about your pet's profile and lifestyle.

  • Step 2

    We will structure a meal plan based on your dog’s breed, weight, age, lifestyle and health challenges.

    Your dog’s health is our priority. For any transition of food, it is important to introduce the food slowly. The transition period will be based on what your dog currently eats.

  • Step 3

    We will send you an email or whatsapp with the recommend food quantity and the subscription options.

    Note:we may call you to understand more about your pet to ensure he is getting the right nutrition.

  • Step 4

    Our freshly made meals will be delivered to you on a weekly basis consisting of 7 meals. Every pack is made ready to serve. Take a pack out of the refrigerator and pour it into your pet's bowl.

    Note: Even though our meals can be stored in the fridge for up to 7 days, due to power cuts in Bangalore, it is recommended that meals 4-7 be stored in the freezer and thaw before serving.
