Rich in antioxidants and high in fibre, chia seeds contain a potent mix of beneficial vitamins and minerals including phosphorus, iron, zinc, potassium and magnesium. Chia seeds are also great at retaining water, so they’ll help to keep your pup hydrated and slow their digestion, which helps with blood sugar and glucose levels.
