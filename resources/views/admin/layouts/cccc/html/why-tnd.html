Importance of Nutrition

Dogs live longer eating real food. We want dogs to spend less time at the vet and more years with you.

It only takes a deficiency in one essential nutrient to pose serious health risks to your dog over time. Most pet parents are unaware of the nutritional requirement. With our 40+ bio active nutrients weekly plan we make sure your dog gets everything they need to thrive.

You are what you eat, and same goes for your dogs. Real food diets have been linked to longer lifespans in dogs.

Good fats translate to a shinier, glossier coat and healthier skin.

Fresh ingredients are easy to digest and gives your dog elevated energy which leads to better and consistent bowel movements!.
